This page is meant to educate the world on these three thorny species. The porcupine is known for its long thorn like spines that are very important for defending themselves against their predators. Hedgehogs also have small thorn like quills on their backs to keep predators off. And finally Cacti are notorious for their venomous thorns that prevent them form being eaten by animals.
